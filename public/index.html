Siraj Center

A Program of the Mountain House Muslim Association (MHMA)

Siraj Center is a California nonprofit religious organization operating under the governance and oversight of the Mountain House Muslim Association (MHMA). Its mission is to support religious, educational, and community-centered initiatives that serve the Muslim community.

Mission

Siraj Center supports religious, educational, and community-centered initiatives that serve the Muslim community in California and beyond.

Governance

Siraj Center is governed by its own Board of Directors and operates under the oversight of the MHMA Board of Trustees, which appoints the Siraj Center Board and ensures alignment with MHMA's mission and fiduciary standards.

Governance & Oversight

Governance Structure

  • • Siraj Center is a separate nonprofit organization.
  • • The MHMA Board of Trustees provides governance oversight.
  • • The MHMA Trustees appoint the Siraj Center Board of Directors.
  • • Siraj Center officers are elected by its Board of Directors.
